Frequently asked questions

How does it work?

You answer a series of guided questions. AI is there to help along the way and then generates a brand identity snapshot tailored to your answers.

What do I get at the end?

A structured summary of your brand’s mission, audience, values, differentiation, tone, and visual direction — clear enough to guide your next steps.

Do I need design or branding experience?

Not at all. The tool is built for entrepreneurs, freelancers, and small business owners who want clarity without hiring a full branding team.

How long does it take?

You can complete the entire process in a couple minutes, but the more you put in the more you will get out. You can move at your own pace and come back anytime.

Is my information private?

Yes. Your responses are yours alone — we don’t share or sell your data.

Can I update my answers later?

Absolutely. You can revisit questions, refine your answers, and generate an updated brand snapshot if anything changes!
